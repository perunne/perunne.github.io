Here is where any information on the UCL Language and Computation reading group will go!

This is a fortnightly(?) reading and presentation group, organised by me (Mika) and Wenkai, where students and faculty can discuss papers, hold talks and present research in an informal setting.

The topics being discussed will generally revolve around the intersections of theoretical computer science and mathematical logic with linguistic theory, such as applications of complexity, computability and information theory to the study of language, but anything goes, so long as its related to linguistics and has a formal or mathematical component to it.

Sessions will be held on Fridays from 3pm to 4pm, with the location changing between sessions. Below is the current schedule for the semester, to be updated once I have some more ideas:
